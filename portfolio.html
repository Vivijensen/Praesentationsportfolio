Portfolio

Welcome to my personal portfolio page! Here, I showcase a carefully curated selection of my projects that reflect my journey over the past 6 months as a multimedia design student. From photography to video editing and digital art – explore the diversity of my work and be inspired by my creative vision. Dive into the world of multimedia design, where each project tells a unique story. Enjoy exploring!

Product Pictures

I love to showcase my creative and aesthetic flair through the lens of photography. My passion lies in presenting products in a visually captivating manner, emphasizing and highlighting their unique features. Following a photo shoot, I bring my images to life through professional editing, utilizing versatile programs like Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop. This post-production phase allows me to refine and enhance the visual impact, ensuring that each photograph tells a compelling story and resonates with its intended audience.

Digital Art

I channel my creative instincts by designing on my iPad through diverse programs like Procreate or Adobe Illustrator. Engaging in this digital canvas, I not only craft personalized hand letterings but also bring forth an array of illustrations tailored to specific needs. These illustrations serve different purposes, finding application in website design and other similar contexts. For instance, one of my projects involved creating a waffle illustration for the redesign of an ice cream café's website, adding a delightful touch to its visual identity. This fusion of creativity and digital proficiency allows me to contribute unique and visually captivating elements to various design projects.

Portrait Photography

My passion lies in portrait photography. Capturing situations visually and setting people in the spotlight brings me great joy. I have already gained experience in newborn photography, as well as family or individual shoots. After the photo shoot, I edit the images in Adobe Lightroom or Adobe Photoshop, making refinements to ensure the overall result is completely satisfying.

Adobe Illustrator Portrait

I've gained a lot of experience using Adobe Illustrator. I've worked on various projects using both the MacBook and iPad versions, using the Apple Pen for more precision. One project I'm proud of is a self-portrait I drew using vector-based drawing. It's been a fun journey learning the ropes of Illustrator and creating cool designs along the way!

Sprite Animation

In Adobe Illustrator, I crafted the logo for my website. It was a two-step process. First, I designed a sprite sheet, essentially a sequence of images that would form the animated logo. Each image captured a different frame of the animation. After finalizing the design, I moved on to the technical side. I generated HTML code that makes the animation possible on my website. This coding magic allows the logo to come alive, adding a playful and dynamic touch.
